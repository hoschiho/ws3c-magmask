Our Story.

Since the pandemic in 2020, people have been using face masks every day. Unfortunately they are still cumbersome and inconvenient to put on and wear. It may not be a big deal to strap them on, but depending on your lifestyle, it might get tiring to wrap them around your ears. Glasses, headphones and especially big earrings make it nearly impossible to put on a mask quickly. That's why we have created MagMask. MagMask is a revolutionary face mask with a metal piece on the strap and it comes with fitting magnet earrings. That way you can easily fix your mask on your earrings and remove it again by just pulling off the mask.
